HR BOT

HR chatbot is developed to make the interview process smoother and easy.

The concept of NLP is used to develop chatbots. In this chatbot, users can give technical exams of particular technologies like Python, Javascript, Game development, etc. The chatbot will automatically verify whether the answer is correct or not. After the examination, the user can know whether he/she is qualified or not.

This chatbot also acts as an AI chatbot and provide details like current hiring, recent projects done by the company, blogs, contact details, etc.

  • Category: Chatbot
  • Technology: Django, AI, ML
